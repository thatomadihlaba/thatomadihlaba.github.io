    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our Humble Beginnings The Mzansi Dance Cypher was conceived as a platform to celebrate and showcase South Africa's diverse dance culture. We started with a vision to create a vibrant event where local talent could shine and engage with audiences. The initial events set the stage for what would become a significant annual highlight in the South African dance community. March 2021 A Premier Competition Emerges In March 2021, the Mzansi Dance Cypher officially launched as a premier dance competition. We provided a dynamic platform for dancers from across South Africa to compete, express their creativity, and gain recognition. The inaugural event was a milestone, drawing significant participation and setting a high standard for future competitions.
